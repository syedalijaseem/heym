# Migrate to Heym

Already have workflows in another tool? **AI Convert** turns them into Heym workflows for you. Paste a workflow from n8n, Flowise, Dify, Langflow, sim.ai, or Activepieces — or just describe what you want — and Heym builds the equivalent workflow on a live canvas you can download and import.

Open it at **[heym.run/convert](https://heym.run/convert)**, or use the **✨ AI Convert** button in the site header.

## How it works

1. **Pick your source tool** (optional) — n8n, Flowise, Dify, Langflow, sim.ai, Activepieces, or leave it on *Auto*.
2. **Provide your workflow** — paste the exported JSON from your current tool, or write a plain-English description of the automation you want.
3. **Generate** — Heym streams the result onto a canvas, drawing nodes and connections as they are produced.
4. **Download or copy** the generated workflow as JSON.
5. **Import into Heym** — use the [Download & Import](../reference/download-import.md) flow to paste the JSON onto your canvas, then run it.

The conversion also adds a small *Sticky Note* on the canvas marking the workflow as generated by Heym — delete it whenever you like.

## What you get

The converter maps source concepts onto native Heym nodes — triggers, [LLM](../nodes/llm-node.md) steps, [HTTP](../nodes/http-node.md) calls, [Slack](../nodes/slack-node.md)/[Telegram](../nodes/telegram-node.md) actions, [Loops](../nodes/loop-node.md), [Conditions](../nodes/condition-node.md), and [Output](../nodes/output-node.md). Review the result on the canvas, wire up your [Credentials](../reference/credentials.md), and adjust anything the source tool expressed differently.

> AI Convert is a starting point, not a guaranteed 1:1 port. Always review the generated workflow before running it in production.

## Good to know

- **AI-powered:** conversions are produced by a large language model, so results can vary between runs. Re-generate if the first result isn't quite right.
- **Input size:** the input you paste or type is limited to roughly **25,000 tokens**. Trim very large exports before converting.
- **Rate limits:** to keep the tool available to everyone, requests are capped at **1 per minute, 5 per hour, and 10 per day** per visitor.
- **Safety:** requests are screened, and only workflow-building requests are processed.
